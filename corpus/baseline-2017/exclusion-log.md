## Audit notes

- **Temporal cutoff:** Any paper with `publication_date > 2017-12-31` was rejected.
  PubMed's publication dates occasionally use `Year` only; in those cases we assigned
  `YYYY-01-01` and the paper passes if `YYYY <= 2017`.
- **Relevance filter:** A scoring heuristic (0-10) based on keyword density, founder authors,
  title keywords, venue type, and MeSH alignment. Threshold was set to 3 to maximize recall
  on this young field (~2012 foundational paper).
- **Deduplication:** Preprint/peer-reviewed duplicates were merged in favor of the
  peer-reviewed version. Title-canonicalized matching handles cases where DOI and PMID
  differ between preprint and publication.
- **Non-English papers:** Excluded in this pass. Budget for translation not allocated.
- **bioRxiv direct search:** Unavailable (Cloudflare challenge on search endpoint, and
  `api.biorxiv.org/details/biorxiv/{range}` returned "Not available at this time" during
  fetch window on 2026-04-23). Europe PMC PPR source
  was used as proxy.

If a reviewer questions "why isn't paper X in the corpus?", grep by PMID / DOI / title
keyword in this log to find the exclusion reason.
