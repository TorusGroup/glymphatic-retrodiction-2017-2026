## Audit notes

- **Temporal window:** `2018-01-01 <= publication_date <= 2026-04-23`.
  Any paper outside was rejected.
- **Relevance filter:** Same scoring heuristic (0-10) as Phase 2. Threshold = 3.
  Deliberately held constant for methodological parity with ≤2017 corpus.
- **Deduplication:** Preprint/peer-reviewed duplicates were merged in favor of the
  peer-reviewed version. Matches by DOI > PMID > canonicalized title.
- **Non-English papers:** Excluded (no translation budget).
- **Query strategy:** Added 5 new queries vs Phase 2 (Alzheimers, Parkinsons, perivascular-space-clinical,
  lymphatic-cns, drug-delivery, anesthesia). Net +5 queries covers post-2018 expansion.
